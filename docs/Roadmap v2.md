# Mythos: Roadmap v2 (The Agentic Era)

## Vision

Mythos v2 transforms from a content generation tool into an **autonomous storytelling agent**. We are moving beyond manual "Create Post" flows into goal-driven, context-aware campaigns.

---

## Tier 6: Intelligence & Context

_The Foundation of Understanding_

- **Phase 6.1: Contextual Brand Indexing**
  - Implement vector indexing for local brand assets, style guides, and past performance.
  - Allow the AI to "know" the brand's history before drafting.
- **Phase 6.2: "Voice Verify" Authenticity Tool**
  - AI-powered authenticity checker to flag generic or "robotic" language.
  - Suggest "Grit & Imperfection" edits to maintain human-like resonance.
- **Phase 6.3: Autonomous Research Worker**
  - Worker that monitors niche-specific trends and news.
  - Auto-scaffolds "Trend Reaction" campaign drafts for review.

---

## Tier 7: Agentic Workflows

_The Move to Autonomy_

- **Phase 7.1: Goal-Driven Campaign Lead**
  - Users define objective (e.g., "Drive 100 signups") rather than posts.
  - AI manages the scheduling, creative pivots, and follow-ups.
- **Phase 7.2: "Research-to-Post" Pipeline**
  - Seamless flow from Research Worker → Copy Generation → Design Optimization.
- **Phase 7.3: Self-Healing Publishing**
  - AI monitors post failures or low engagement and auto-proposes status-quo changes or rescheduling.

---

## Tier 8: The Creator Suite (Anti-Corporate)

_Competitive Differentiation_

- **Phase 8.1: Deeplink Publishing (Native Handoff)**
  - Desktop-to-Mobile flow for TikTok/Instagram.
  - Preserves native music, stickers, and editing while speeding up drafting.
- **Phase 8.2: Community CRM (Superfan Insights)**
  - Merge vendor metadata (who comments/shares) with backend user data.
  - Tag "Superfans" to prioritize engagement and customer support.
- **Phase 8.3: "Grain & Grit" Visual Engine**
  - Image generation defaults optimized for organic, photographic aesthetics (Flux/Midjourney v6 styles).

---

## Tier 9: Distribution & Ecosystem

_Scaling the Business_

- **Phase 9.1: Workflow Integrated Plugins**
  - Canva/Framer/Photoshop integration for asset sync.
- **Phase 9.2: Monetization & Organizations**
  - Lemon Squeezy integration for billing.
  - Fine-grained permissions for agency/collaborative workflows.
- **Phase 9.3: Commercial Safety & License Tracking**
  - Automatic license tagging for AI-generated assets.
